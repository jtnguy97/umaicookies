About



The Name

Umai (うまい, pronounced "ooh-my") is a Japanese word that can be translated to "good" or "delicious". The company's name was inspired by the character Rengoku from an anime called Demon Slayers. He was known for always saying the word "umai" after he took a bite of food to express its deliciousness. But there was also deep meaning to his actions. In Japan, samurai were trained to relish and enjoy every bite of their food because it might be their last meal. We hope our cookies can provide a moment of bliss as you enjoy each bite!


The Mission

umai cookies was founded by Trinh Tran after much encouragement and support from her friends and family. She has been making her signature Dark Chocolate Toffee cookies for gatherings and lactation cookies for her nursing cousins for years. Realizing the impact lactation cookies would make for nursing mothers struggling with low breastmilk supply or inflated formula prices, she decided to launch umai cookies. The lactation cookies are TT Cookies™ to make it easier to refer to and were named after her name and well... breasts. Our aim is to provide products that are just good: focusing on high quality ingredients and playful flavor profiles.
